# MASTER STYLE BIBLE – Globalny Stylesheet

> Ten plik definiuje styl, który musi pojawić się w KAŻDYM prompcie. Kopiuj blok "STYLE CORE" do każdego promptu.

## 🎨 Docelowy styl komiksu
"Dark fantasy surrealism with steampunk elements, diorama aesthetic on cork board, cinematic lighting, detailed yet stylized, inspired by Thorgal, Norse mythology meets AI meta-narrative"

## 👨‍🎨 Referencje artystyczne (max 3)
1. Thorgal (Grzegorz Rosiński) - nordic fantasy
2. Moebius - surrealism, diorama compositions
3. Steampunk aesthetic - brass machinery, gears

## ✍️ Technika rysunku
- Lineart: Clean to semi-sketchy, detailed
- Shading: Cinematic, dramatic, volumetric lighting
- Detaliczność: High detail (mechanizmy, runy, tekstury)
- Tekstury: Cork board background, brass/bronze metallic, weathered wood, glowing crystals

## 💡 LIGHTING SYSTEM
- Primary: Chiaroscuro (hard contrast)
- Secondary: God rays from above
- Ambient: Perpetual dusk / moonlight
- Magic: Volumetric cyan glow (#2dd4bf)

## ✍️ LINEART & TEXTURE
- Line weight: Medium to heavy (comic style)
- Shading: Cel-shaded + soft airbrush
- Texture: Cork board + brass metallic + weathered wood

## 🔥 STYLE CORE KEYWORDS
"dark urban fantasy noir, cinematic chiaroscuro lighting, perpetual dusk, god rays, volumetric fog, detailed yet stylized, cork board diorama aesthetic, steampunk elements, Norse mythology meets AI meta-narrative"


## 📐 Kompozycja i kadrowanie
- Preferowany shot size: Wide shots 40% (epic scenes), medium 35%, close-up 25%
- Kąt kamery: Low angle (heroes), eye-level (dialogue), dutch tilt (tension)
- **UNIKALNY ELEMENT: Cork board diorama frame** - każdy obraz "przybity" do korkowej tablicy z elementami dekoracyjnymi (runy, maszyny, świece, zwoje)

## 🖼️ ESTETYKA DIORAMY (critical!)
- Cork board texture background
- Push pins / clips holding the image
- Decorative elements around frame: brass flying machines, scrolls, runes, candles, gears
- 3D diorama effect - image looks like it's pinned to a board
- Mix of ancient (runes, Vikings) and steampunk (brass machinery)

## 🚫 Negatywy – czego unikać
style shift, different artist, blurry face, deformed hands, extra limbs, realistic photo, 3d render (unless specified), chibi, anime eyes, modern technology (except intentional anachronisms like shopping cart), clean minimalism (we want detailed diorama)

## 🔗 Globalne parametry techniczne
--ar 3:4 (portrait scenes) or 16:9 (epic wide scenes)
--style raw
--cw 100
--stylize 300-400 (higher for surreal elements)

## 📎 Dekonstrukcja estetyki dioramy (dla AI)
Composition: Main image centered, framed by cork board texture
Border elements: brass flying machines, scrolls with runes, candles, gears, push pins
Effect: Looks like a museum display / detective board / collector's diorama
Mix: Ancient Norse + steampunk + surreal time elements
